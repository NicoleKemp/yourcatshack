About Us

Your Cat Shack was established on 1 April 2016, by myself, Yvonne Rabeling. Over the past few years we have had many happy cat campers pass through our doors. Your Cat Shack started as a dream which soon turned into a reality after I became unemployed. I have no staff on board, I personally care for and attend to all the cats boarding at Your Cat Shack.


We are very passionate about animals and have many pet residents living with us, two very spoilt Rottweilers, Baretta & Calibre, many tropical fish and two adopted parrots Joey the Sun Conure and Loki the African Senegal.

Accommodation

Cats are housed in large, spacious and very comfortable duplex enclosures. We will never put strange cats together, however the enclosures are large enough to accommodate up to 3 cats should you have more than one cat that are happy to live in each others company. We supply all their comforts such as beds, blankets, toys as well as litter and trays. You as the owner need to supply their food needs.
